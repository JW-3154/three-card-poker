Welcome to Three Card Poker! Here are the game rules:
1. In Three Card Poker, hand rankings from lowest to highest are: 
High Card, Pair, Flush, Straight, Three of a Kind, Straight Flush.
2. You must place an ante bet to start the game.
3. Minimum ante bet is $100, maximum cannot exceed half of your balance.
4. After placing the ante, you may choose to add a Pair Plus side bet.
   Pair Plus payout rates:
        Pair: 1 to 1
        Flush: 4 to 1 (3 to 1 if it's California mode)
        Straight: 6 to 1
        Three of a Kind: 30 to 1
        Straight Flush: 40 to 1
        Mini Royal Flush: 200 to 1 (California mode only)
5. After cards are dealt, if you choose to compare hands, 
you must place a play bet equal to your ante.
If you fold, the game ends and you lose your ante.
6. Winning conditions:
   If you enter the comparison phase:
   A. If the dealer does not have at least Queen-high or better, you automatically win.
   Your ante and play bet are returned, and you win the dealer's ante.
   B. If the dealer qualifies and your hand is stronger than the dealer's or has the same rank but higher cards,
   you win.Your ante and play bet are returned, and you win the dealer's ante and play bet.
   Otherwise, you lose both your ante and play bet.
   C. Ante bonus payouts for special hands:
        Straight: 1 to 1
        Three of a Kind: 4 to 1
        Straight Flush: 5 to 1
7. If hands are identical in both rank and card values, it's a push - 
your ante and play bet are returned.
8. If you exit the game midway, any placed bets will not be refunded.
